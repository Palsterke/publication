Some words about me and my work

I’m currently a student at the LUCA school of arts, where I specify in the art of painting. My work exist solely out of personal photographs of animals and plants. I love the mystery of accidental finds in forests or pictures. These mysteries can’t be solved for even I do not know what they mean.
